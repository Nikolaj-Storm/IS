What we can do for you

Utilization of idle periods

At any given time, 7.3% of retail stores in Central Copenhagen stand empty, losing millions in potential revenue for landlords every single month. And this is not just a temporary issue, according to Ejendom Danmark, national idle periods have risen almost 200% since 2000. This is largely due to the rise of e-commerce. But, the data shows that there is still tremendous value to be gained for e-commerce companies from having physical contact with their customers through pop-up shops. We want to connect your retail spaces that are temporarily empty, with companies looking for temporary physical locations. This will show other potential renters the possibilities of the space, connect you with our companies (who could be potential long-term renters), and most importantly put money in your pockets by utilizing idle periods.
